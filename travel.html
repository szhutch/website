TRAVEL

The world is a very different place to this time last year. I am grateful for the chance I had to travel across the world last summer visiting Singapore, Sydney and Fiji. Also last November I went to visit a friend in Berlin. Here are some photos of my travels. I would love to travel some more in the future when time, money and global health allows for it.

BERLIN

I went to Berlin in November 2019 to visit my friend who was living and working there. I had been once before on a school art trip back in 2016. It was great going at this time of year as we managed to go to a Christmas Market at a palace.

FIJI

I spent a whole month living and volunteering in Fiji in August-September 2019. I participated in a project with CAUKIN Studio. Apart from helping to build a kindergarten I went fishing, snorkelling and explored the local area.

SYDNEY

I spent a week in Sydney on the way to Fiji. It was fun and I'd like to go back one day to see more of Australia. I stayed in a hostel and made some other traveller friends whilst there. I also went on a hike in the Blue Mountains with a tour group called Emu Trekkers whose proceeds go towards the Indigenous Literacy Fund.

SINGAPORE

I only spent 3 days in Singapore but I managed to see a lot in that time! It felt so futuristic and green and clean compared to anywhere I'd ever been before. I loved visiting the Gardens by the Bay and watching a light show amongst the giant artificial trees.

ABOUT ME

I was working as an Architectural Assistant in London but have been made redundant due to COVID-19. I am currently doing the 3 week Web Developer Coding Course by Code Nation and this is my first time coding and making a website. I have decided to use it as a platform to showcase some of my interests; food, travel and architecture. Take a look around! Hope you enjoy :)
